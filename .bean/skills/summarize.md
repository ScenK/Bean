# Summarize

Read the provided content (a dropped URL, a file, or pasted text) and produce a tight summary:

- One-sentence TL;DR at the top.
- 3–6 bullet points covering the key ideas.
- Call out anything surprising, risky, or actionable.

Keep it short. Prefer plain language over jargon.
